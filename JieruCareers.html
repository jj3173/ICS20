Software Designer

Computer Software Designer's implenment/develop applications by using science and mathematics. They can create a variety of programs ranging from video games to operating systemes. They create these programs to satisfy the needs of their consumers.

  • Companies/Organizations hiring: HB Studios Multimedia
  • Programming Language/Software needed:CSS, HTML, C++, C#, Lua, JavaScript, SQL, and other 3D graphics programming (Shaders, DirectX, OpenGL)
  • Level of education:Computer Science (or similar) Graduate, and two years of relevant experience, Fundamental math skills (matrices, vectors, algebra, etc)
  • Experience: Working in a Game Development Company: 3 years, Full Game Cycle experience Start to finish with commercial release: 1 year, Experience working on video games and with Windows programming

Product Designer

Product Designer's combine science, arts and technology to create new products. They also use their technical knowledge to improve and modify already made products to improve their functionality and aesthetic appeal.

  • Companies/Organizations hiring:Found People Inc., Zoocasa, Myplanet
  • Programming Language/Software needed:Sketch, Adobe Creative Suite, InVision, Keynote, Pixate, Omnigraffle, Arduino/Particle, Processing, HTML/CSS/LESS/SASS, Javascript, Framer.js
  • Level of education:Degree in HCI, Design, or equivalent experience
  • Experience:3+ years professional experience with web and/or mobile design, Product Design: 2 years
